Privacy Policy - TikSaver

Last updated: 2026-02-07

1. Data We Collect

We process limited technical and account-related data to provide app functionality:

  • Device identifier generated by the app
  • Internal user identifier
  • TikTok account link data entered by the user (for account linking flow)
  • Download/usage counters for free-tier limits
  • Subscription purchase tokens and entitlement status (for restore and billing state)

2. Why We Use Data

  • Authenticate app requests and prevent abuse/rate-limit bypass
  • Associate user account with device and subscription status
  • Enable premium access and purchase restore
  • Provide core download/format features

3. Storage and Retention

Data is stored on backend infrastructure and in local app storage where required for functionality. We retain data as long as necessary to operate the service and comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play Billing for subscriptions and restoration of purchases. These services process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. If we learn that personal data from a child under 13 has been collected without appropriate consent, we will delete it.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
